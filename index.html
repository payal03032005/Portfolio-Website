My Journey

I completed my schooling at Kendriya Vidyalaya Gangtok, Sikkim, where I consistently scored above 90% throughout my academic journey. In Class 10, I achieved 93.4%. Surprisingly, my childhood dream was not related to technology at all—I wanted to become a cardiologist, and Mathematics was one of my least favorite subjects.

After Class 10, my father encouraged me to pursue engineering. His belief in education came from his own life experiences. He left home at a young age, completed a Diploma in Electrical Engineering, worked multiple jobs including driving autos and trucks, and eventually built his career through determination and hard work. His struggles taught me the value of education, discipline, and perseverance.

I secured 88 percentile in JEE and received admission to an Electronics & Telecommunication program. However, I wanted a path that aligned more closely with my interests. Later, I joined Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology & Research, Amravati in the Industrial IoT branch.

The first two years of engineering were focused mainly on academics. I maintained a strong CGPA but had not yet discovered my true interests. My routine was simple: attend college, study for exams, and repeat. Deep down, I felt I could do much more.

Everything changed when I started exploring technology beyond the classroom. I discovered Data Analytics and began learning Python, SQL, and Power BI. Building my first analytics project helped me realize how technology and data can solve real-world problems.

During my sixth semester, I faced an unexpected challenge when I was diagnosed with gallbladder stones and underwent surgery. Instead of allowing this period to slow me down, I used my recovery time to learn Data Structures & Algorithms, build projects, improve my resume, and strengthen my technical skills.

Today, I am passionate about Data Engineering, ETL Development, Cloud Technologies, Database Systems, and Data Analytics. From someone who once dreamed of becoming a doctor and disliked mathematics, I have grown into a developer who enjoys building applications, analyzing data, and continuously learning new technologies.

"Success is not about having everything figured out from the beginning. It's about staying curious and taking one step forward every day."
